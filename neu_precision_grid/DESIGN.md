# Design System Theme

## Overview
This document outlines the core design tokens and aesthetic principles that govern the user interface. It ensures consistency and brand identity across all digital products.

## Color Palette
The color palette is meticulously crafted to define the visual tone and hierarchy within the interface.

-   **Color Mode:** dark
-   **Primary Color:** `#0A192F` - This deep, rich color serves as the brand's cornerstone, ideal for prominent interactive elements and calls to action.
-   **Secondary Color:** `#708090` - A cool, understated gray, providing support for less emphasized UI components, subtle distinctions, and secondary information.
-   ****Tertiary Color:** `#00D2FF` - A vibrant and bright blue, used as an accent for highlights, badges, and to draw attention to specific, important details.
-   **Neutral Color:** `#0A192F` - A dark neutral color, forming the foundation for backgrounds, surfaces, and non-chromatic elements, ensuring readability and focus on content.

## Typography
Typography plays a crucial role in conveying information and establishing the brand's voice.

-   **Headline Font:** Manrope - A modern, geometric sans-serif, chosen for its clarity and impact in headlines.
-   **Body Font:** Inter - A highly legible and versatile font, optimized for on-screen reading and used for all extensive body text.
-   **Label Font:** Inter - Consistent with the body font for labels, ensuring coherence and readability across interactive elements.

## Shape and Form
The handling of corners and spacing defines the tactile feel of the interface.

-   **Roundedness:** Subtle roundedness (1) - UI elements feature slightly softened corners, offering a refined yet approachable aesthetic.
-   **Spacing:** Normal (2) - A balanced approach to whitespace, providing adequate breathing room between elements for clarity and ease of use, without appearing overly sparse or dense.

## Thematic Interpretation
The "fidelity" color variant has been chosen, emphasizing precision and trust, aligning with a professional and reliable user experience. This theme combines a dark aesthetic with a focus on clear information hierarchy and a clean, modern feel.
