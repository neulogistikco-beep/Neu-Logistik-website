Executive Summary This document outlines the scope, goals, and requirements for developing an AI-Powered Content Suggestion Engine within our existing [Platform Name] content creation suite. Problem Statement Our users, primarily [Target Audience - e.g., small business owners, marketers, bloggers], frequently express challenges with: * **Writer's Block:** Struggling to come up with new ideas or angles for content, leading to frustration and delays. * **Time Constraints:** Spending excessive time on brainstorming and research before starting content creation. * **Content Consistency:** Difficulty maintaining a consistent flow of fresh, engaging content due to idea generation fatigue. * **Low Engagement/Churn:** Users abandoning content creation mid-way or churning due to a perceived lack of tools to support their creative process. This leads to a suboptimal user experience and limits the full potential of our platform for content creators. --- ### 3. Goals & Objectives **Overall Business Goal:** Increase user retention and subscription value by enhancing the core content creation experience. **Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound (SMART) Objectives (V1):** * **Increase Feature Adoption:** Achieve a 20% weekly active usage rate for the content suggestion feature among eligible users within 3 months post-launch. * **Boost Content Creation:** Increase the average number of published content pieces per active user by 10% within 6 months post-launch. * **Improve User Satisfaction:** Achieve an average User Satisfaction Score (CSAT) of 4.5/5 for the content suggestion feature (measured via in-app surveys) within 4 months post-launch. * **Reduce Time-to-Draft:** Reduce the average time taken from "New Content" creation to "First Draft Complete" by 15% for users utilizing suggestions, measured via telemetry. --- ### 4. Target Audience * **Primary:** Existing active users of our [Platform Name] content creation tools who regularly produce articles, blog posts, social media updates, or marketing copy. * **Secondary:** New users onboarding to the platform who are looking for tools to streamline their content workflow. --- ### 5. Solution Overview / Product Description The AI-Powered Content Suggestion Engine will be an integrated feature within our existing content editor. Users will be able to: 1. Input a short seed topic, keyword, or paragraph. 2. Receive multiple relevant content suggestions (e.g., topic ideas, outline points, rephrased sentences, relevant keywords). 3. Select, edit, or incorporate these suggestions directly into their draft. 4. Provide feedback on the quality and relevance of suggestions to help refine the AI model over time. This feature will leverage state-of-the-art Natural Language Processing (NLP) models, either via third-party APIs (e.g., OpenAI, Google AI) or a custom-trained model, to deliver intelligent and creative output. --- ### 6. Key Features (V1 Scope) * **Suggestion Input Field:** A dedicated area in the editor or a modal where users can type a seed topic (min. 3 words, max. 150 characters). * **Multiple Suggestion Types:** * **Topic Ideas:** 3-5 distinct content ideas related to the seed topic. * **Outline Prompts:** 3-5 bullet points that could form an article outline. * **Sentence Rephrasing:** Option to highlight text and request alternative phrasing. * **Interactive Display:** Suggestions presented clearly, allowing users to: * Click to insert into the editor at the cursor. * Copy to clipboard. * Discard/Delete individual suggestions. * **Feedback Mechanism:** Simple "Thumbs Up / Thumbs Down" icon next to each suggestion to gather user sentiment. * **Usage Tracking:** Telemetry to log requests, accepted suggestions, and feedback. * **Rate Limiting:** Backend mechanisms to prevent abuse and manage API costs (if applicable). --- ### 7. Out of Scope (V1) * **Full Content Generation:** The feature will provide *suggestions*, not generate entire articles or paragraphs automatically without user interaction. * **Image Generation/Suggestions:** Focus solely on text-based content. * **Multi-language Support:** V1 will be English-only. * **Real-time SEO Optimization:** While suggestions may contain keywords, full-fledged SEO analysis and optimization are out of scope for V1. * **Advanced AI Personalization:** Beyond basic context from user input, deep personalization based on past user content or style is a future iteration. * **Monetization:** V1 will be included in existing subscription tiers. --- ### 8. User Stories **As a content creator...** * ...I want to input a topic into the editor, so I can get new content ideas quickly. * ...I want to receive several different suggestions (topic, outline, rephrasing), so I have options to choose from. * ...I want to be able to insert a suggestion directly into my draft, so I don't have to copy-paste manually. * ...I want to provide feedback on suggestions (thumbs up/down), so the AI can learn what's useful. * ...I want the suggestions to be relevant to my input, so I don't waste time sifting through irrelevant ideas. * ...I want the suggestion interface to be non-intrusive and easy to use, so it enhances my workflow rather than disrupting it. --- ### 9.
